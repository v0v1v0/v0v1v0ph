auditory R Documentation

Auditory Discrimination Dataset

Description

This dataset is from a study to assess auditory differences between environmental sounds given several other factors.

Usage

data(auditory)

Format

This data frame consists of 3 variables on 20 subjects:

  • pre.test The subject's pre-test score.

  • gain The gain in auditory score between the pre- and post-test administration of the treatment.

  • Culture A cultural status indicator of individuals, where 1 means the subject is from a "culturally-nondeprived" group and 0 means the subject is from a "culturally-deprived" group.
