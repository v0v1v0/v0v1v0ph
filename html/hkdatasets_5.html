hkstreetnames20 R Documentation

Dataset with Hong Kong Street Names as at 2020.

Description

A dataset containing street names in Hong Kong as at 2020.

Usage

hkstreetnames20

Format

A data frame with 4603 rows and 21 variables:

DC

Constituency code for the District Council

StreetNames_EN

Street name in English

StreetNames_ZH

Street name in Chinese

TM

Tuen Mun district

ST

Sha Tin district

E

Eastern district

S

Southern district

WC

Wan Chai district

C&W

Central and Western district

Is

Islands district

YL

Yuen Long district

SK

Sai Kung district

KC

Kowloon City district

YTM

Yau Tsim Mong district

KT

Kwun Tong district

SSP

Sham Shui Po district

N

North district

TP

Tai Po district

K&T

Kwai Tsing district

TW

WTS
